Food

What to eat

  • Try schnitzel, goulash, apfelstrudel and sachertorte.
  • Balance one traditional meal with bakery and supermarket food because Vienna is one of the costlier stops.
  • Daily allowance: £28-£40 per day.
Activities

Best use of time

  • Schonbrunn Palace: Pre-book one timed tour; allow roughly £25-£35 depending on the ticket.
  • Belvedere: Choose it for Klimt and art; the exterior gardens can be enough on a tight budget.
  • St Stephen's Cathedral: The main church has free areas; the tower and catacombs cost extra.
  • Ringstrasse: Use normal trams and walking instead of a generic bus tour.
