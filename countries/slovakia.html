Food

What to eat

  • Try bryndzove halusky, garlic soup and lokse.
  • Use a lunch menu and eat one street away from the busiest Old Town squares.
  • Daily allowance: £22-£30 per day.
Activities

Best use of time

  • Old Town and Michael's Gate: Compact and easy to cover on foot.
  • Blue Church: The exterior is free; interior access depends on services and opening.
  • Bratislava Castle: Climb for Danube views; the grounds are free and museum entry costs extra.
  • UFO viewpoint: Optional; skip it in fog or when the budget is tight.
