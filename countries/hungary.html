Food

What to eat

  • Try langos, chicken paprikash, chimney cake and stew.
  • Use markets, bakeries and lunch menus; eat away from Vaci Street for better value.
  • Daily allowance: £22-£32 per day.
Activities

Best use of time

  • Buda Castle district: Combine Fisherman's Bastion, Matthias Church exterior and Danube viewpoints.
  • Parliament and riverfront: The exterior is excellent; allow £20-£35 for an official interior tour.
  • Thermal bath: Allow two to three hours and about £25-£40; carry swimwear and check locker/towel charges.
  • Danube night view: Walk or use ordinary public transport before paying for a cruise.
