Day by day

Complete six-day itinerary

Daily figures total £1,440 before contingency.

Day1

London → Istanbul

Istanbul night 1
  • Take an early London flight and transfer to Sultanahmet or Sirkeci.
  • Visit the Blue Mosque, Sultanahmet Square and Hagia Sophia.
  • If arrival time permits, add a short Bosphorus cruise.

Blue Mosque is free but closes to visitors during prayer. Dress respectfully.

Planned day cost£340Flight, room, food, activities and local travel
Day2

Istanbul → Cappadocia

Cappadocia night 1
  • Fly to Kayseri (ASR) or Nevşehir (NAV), then take a pre-booked Göreme shuttle.
  • Visit Göreme Open Air Museum and walk through Göreme.
  • Join the ATV sunset tour. Book the balloon for the first available morning.

Balloon flights are weather-dependent, so the first morning gives you a backup chance.

Planned day cost£284Flight, room, food, activities and shuttle
Day3

Cappadocia activity day

Cappadocia night 2
  • Take the sunrise hot-air balloon flight.
  • Join a North/Red Tour covering Uçhisar, Love Valley, Paşabağ, Avanos and Devrent.
  • Confirm whether lunch and attraction entries are included, then prepare for the early Day 4 bus.

The two-night plan is more comfortable. An overnight bus would save a room but does not match this itinerary.

Planned day cost£345Room, food, balloon and Red Tour
Day4

Göreme → Antalya

Antalya night 1
  • Take an early coach to Antalya; the journey is normally 7 hours 20 minutes to 9 hours.
  • After check-in, explore Kaleiçi, Hadrian's Gate, the marina and Hıdırlık Tower.
  • Finish the evening at Karaalioğlu Park.

This is mainly a travel day. Do not book a timed afternoon activity.

Planned day cost£148Bus, room, food and local transport
Day5

Pamukkale day trip

Antalya night 2
  • Leave Antalya around 05:00-06:00 and travel through Denizli.
  • Allow five to six hours for the travertines, Hierapolis, Roman Theatre and museum.
  • Add Cleopatra Antique Pool only if time allows, then return to Antalya late evening.

This is a 14-16-hour day. Check the last return coach before buying the outbound ticket.

Planned day cost£181Return buses, room, food, entry and local travel
Day6

Antalya → London

No room
  • Choose one morning option: Konyaaltı Beach, Upper Düden or Tünektepe Cable Car.
  • Travel to Antalya Airport at least three hours before the international departure.
  • Fly direct to London.

Do not try to fit all three morning options around an early flight.

Planned day cost£142Flight, food, activity and airport travel
