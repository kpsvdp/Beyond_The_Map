Base: Tirana

Flight plan: London to Tirana (TIA), direct return flight

Hotel plan: Near Skanderbeg Square or Blloku; 2 nights

Three-day itinerary

Day What I will do Transport and buses
Day 1
Arrival and central Tirana		 I will take the Rinas Express airport bus, check in, then walk Skanderbeg Square, Et'hem Bey Mosque, the New Bazaar and Blloku. Airport bus; walking; city bus if needed.
Day 2
History and Dajti		 I will visit Bunk'Art 2 or the House of Leaves, then use a city bus and cable car for Mount Dajti. I will return before dark. City bus to Dajti Ekspres; cable car.
Day 3
Krujë half-day and departure		 If my flight is late enough, I will take a minibus to Krujë for the bazaar and castle; otherwise I will keep the morning in Tirana. Regional minibus from the appropriate terminal; airport bus after bag collection.

Transport, local travel and buses

  • Airport: the Rinas Express bus is the budget option; I will confirm

    its central stop and operating hours.

  • Local: Tirana buses are inexpensive but can be slow in traffic. The

    centre is compact enough for most sightseeing on foot.

  • Regional bus: Krujë minibuses do not operate like a fixed UK coach

    timetable, so I will allow slack and drop the excursion if the return risks my flight.

Hotel, food and extra activities

Food plan: I will reserve £17–£28 per day. Two good-value examples are byrek or soup for lunch and chicken, vegetable casserole or qofte with salad for dinner.

Extra activity 1: Dajti Ekspres return ride: allow £12–£18 plus city-bus fare.

Extra activity 2: Bunk'Art 2 or House of Leaves: allow £6–£10 for one museum.
