Activities

Best use of time

  • Charles Bridge: Go early, then continue uphill through Mala Strana.
  • Prague Castle: Grounds and views work for a short visit; allow £15-£22 for a main paid circuit.
  • Old Town Square: See the Astronomical Clock exterior before collecting luggage for Vienna.
