Three Paris nights and the first Schengen entry

Paris anchors the six-day trip. The route enters through Charles de Gaulle, includes the Louvre, Eiffel Tower, Montmartre and a Brussels day trip, then continues by direct train to Amsterdam.

Paris itinerary

Day-by-day details

DAY 1

Arrival, Louvre and Seine

Fly Newcastle to Paris, transfer from CDG, check in, lunch near the hotel, Louvre timed entry, Seine cruise and an evening Eiffel Tower view. If the flight slips, move the Louvre to Day 2.

Arrival day
DAY 2

Paris landmark circuit

Morning walk or cycle, Eiffel Tower, Sacré-Cœur and Montmartre, Moulin Rouge exterior, Notre-Dame area and Pont Alexandre III.

Full day
DAY 3

Brussels day trip

Depart from Gare du Nord, return from Brussels-Midi the same evening and stay in the same Paris hotel. Keep both train reservations in the visa file.

Rail return
DAY 4

Final Paris stops and Amsterdam train

Early Eiffel or Trocadéro view, Galeries Lafayette rooftop, then direct Eurostar to Amsterdam. Arrive at least 20 minutes before the train.

Room

Paris accommodation

  • Stay: 3 continuous nights; historical total £196.
  • Area: Gare du Nord, 10th or 11th can simplify the Brussels train and local travel; still check safety, late-night access and reviews.
  • Visa proof: full guest name, property address, check-in/out dates, booking number and cancellation terms.
  • Belgium night: none. Keep the Paris room active while taking the day trip.
Transport

Flights, rail and Metro

  • NCL → CDG: historical website fare £97.70; flight time about 1h 25m.
  • Airport: Paris Region airport ticket was listed at €14; confirm the terminal and current fare.
  • City: single Metro–Train–RER ticket €2.55 or Navigo Day €12.30, excluding airport travel.
  • Onward: Paris–Amsterdam direct Eurostar about 3h 20m, plan £35–£110.
Food

Paris meal plan

  • Day 1: croissant and coffee, vegetarian or chicken baguette, ratatouille or chicken dinner; £30–£40.
  • Day 2: omelette or quiche, vegetable crêpe, falafel or chicken meal; £35–£45.
  • Day 4: bakery breakfast and station meal before Amsterdam; share of the mixed-day allowance.
