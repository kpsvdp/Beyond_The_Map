Your route in Slovenia

DAY 10

Arrival in Ljubljana

Check in, then walk Preseren Square, Triple Bridge, the riverfront and Dragon Bridge. Keep the evening light after the long coach.

Full Ljubljana day

Visit Central Market, the cathedral area, Ljubljana Castle, Cobblers' Bridge and Tivoli Park. Add Metelkova only if time and energy allow.

Ljubljana to Budapest

Rooms

Accommodation

  • Booking: Budget hostel estimate £25-£45 per night (£50-£90 total)
  • Visa proof: show your full name, property address, dates, confirmation number and payment/cancellation status.
  • Check: city tax, towels, lockers, late arrival and luggage-storage costs before paying.
Transport

Flights, trains and buses

  • Budapest to Ljubljana direct coach: allow 6-7.5 hours and £25-£40.
  • Ljubljana to Budapest direct coach: allow 6-7.5 hours and £25-£40; travel the day before the flight.
  • Central Ljubljana is walkable; allow £4-£7 per day if using buses and an Urbana card.
Food

What to eat

  • Try struklji, jota, potica and local bakery food.
  • Use a bakery breakfast, market or set lunch and only one more expensive riverside dinner.
  • Daily allowance: £24-£34 per day.
Activities

Best use of time

  • Preseren Square and Triple Bridge: Start here and follow the river to Central Market and Dragon Bridge.
  • Ljubljana Castle: Walk up free or allow £12-£18 for funicular and exhibitions.
  • Central Market: A free daytime stop for snacks and local atmosphere.
  • Tivoli Park: An easy free break from the Old Town when the weather is dry.
  • Metelkova: Optional evening walk; stay aware of the area and return route.
