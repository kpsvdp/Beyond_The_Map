Base: Athens

Flight plan: London to Athens (ATH), direct return flight

Hotel plan: Koukaki, Monastiraki or Syntagma; 2 nights

Three-day itinerary

Day What I will do Transport and buses
Day 1
Arrival and historic centre		 I will use the airport Metro or express bus, then walk Syntagma, Plaka, Anafiotika and Monastiraki. I will view the Acropolis at sunset from a nearby hill. Airport Metro/bus; walking.
Day 2
Acropolis and coast		 I will enter the Acropolis early, visit the Acropolis Museum, then spend the afternoon at the National Garden or take a sunset coach to Cape Sounion. Metro and trolleybus; KTEL or organised coach to Sounion.
Day 3
Agora and departure		 I will visit the Ancient Agora or Central Market, eat an early lunch and return to ATH. Metro or airport express bus.

Transport, local travel and buses

  • Airport: the Metro is simple for Syntagma/Monastiraki; 24-hour express

    buses can be better for very late flights.

  • Local: OASA tickets cover Metro, trams, trolleybuses and city buses,

    but the airport products have separate rules.

  • Regional bus: Cape Sounion requires a KTEL/organised coach; I will

    confirm the current stop and return time before leaving.

Hotel, food and extra activities

Food plan: I will reserve £25–£40 per day. Two affordable examples are a souvlaki or spanakopita lunch and a taverna dinner with mezze, chicken, vegetables or moussaka.

Extra activity 1: Cape Sounion sunset excursion: allow £30–£55 including transport and entry.

Extra activity 2: Acropolis Museum and archaeological sites: allow £25–£45 depending on ticket combination.
