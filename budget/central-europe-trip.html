Financial proof examples

Practical funds target

  • If the £715 core is already paid and about £900-£1,250 remains, keep roughly £1,300-£1,800 or more in stable accessible funds after normal bills.
  • If most of the £1,600-£2,100 trip remains unpaid, keep roughly £2,000-£2,500 or more after normal bills.
  • These are planning targets, not official minimums or approval guarantees.
Hard rules

Do not fake the budget

  • A stable balance and matching salary credits are stronger than a sudden deposit.
  • Do not treat a credit-card limit as your savings.
  • Keep enough emergency money for one hotel night, one new intercity ticket and airport transport.
  • Re-price the Budapest-Newcastle flight after confirming the Slovenia extension.
