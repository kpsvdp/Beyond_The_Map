Final money checklist

Do not leave these outside the bank balance

Visa and service fee

Adult Schengen fee is €90. Add the live Italy VFS service fee; optional premium services are not required.

Insurance

Buy Schengen cover of at least €30,000 and verify that paragliding and scuba are included.

Baggage and seats

Low-cost fares may add cabin baggage, checked baggage, seat and payment charges.

City and property charges

Budget for city tax, deposits, lockers, towels and late-check-in charges.

Accessible funds

Show stable money that covers the real unpaid trip cost and normal UK commitments, not only the legal reference amount.

Credit cards

Do not treat an unused credit limit as savings. Use personal current or savings statements with visible transactions.
