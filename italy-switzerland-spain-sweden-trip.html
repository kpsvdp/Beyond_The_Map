High-risk connections

Do not ignore these checks

  • Mountain day: check weather, gondola operation, last descent and activity cancellation terms on the morning.
  • Overnight ferry: confirm terminal, boarding cut-off, baggage allowance and whether the fare includes a seat or cabin.
  • Stockholm arrival: obtain written late-check-in confirmation and pre-plan the after-midnight airport transfer.
  • Krakow self-transfer: separate tickets mean no automatic protection if the first flight is late.
Booking order

Complete the plan in this sequence

  1. Replace the old dates. Check every flight, train, ferry and hostel against a new travel window.
  2. Protect the route. Allow realistic buffers, especially in Rome, Stockholm and Krakow.
  3. Confirm adventure cover. The insurance must include paragliding and scuba diving, not only ordinary sightseeing.
  4. Build one visa file. Use Italy because it has five nights, the longest stay.
  5. Save offline copies. Keep tickets, insurance, rooms, passport and emergency contacts available without internet.
