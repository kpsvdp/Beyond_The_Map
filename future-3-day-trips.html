17 separate trips

The combined £8,060–£14,895 figure is a comparison total, not one itinerary. Every country remains its own 3-day / 2-night London trip.

Planning assumptions

  • Every trip will start from a London airport and will use three

    calendar days with two hotel nights.

  • The budget is for one adult in a simple private room with cabin

    baggage. A hostel bed can reduce the total; a checked bag or premium hotel will raise it.

  • Flight figures are planning ranges, not live quotations. They assume

    shoulder-season travel booked roughly 6–10 weeks ahead. School holidays, summer weekends and last-minute bookings can add 25–60%.

  • The base total includes flights, two hotel nights, airport and local

    transport, buses, food, one or two paid activities and a contingency. Visa/application costs are shown separately.

  • A three-day plan works best when the outbound flight arrives before

    midday and the return flight leaves after 17:00. Otherwise the sightseeing time will drop sharply.

Hard truth: Greenland and Western Sahara are technically possible but poor choices for only three days from London. Connections will consume most of Day 1 and Day 3, and disruption risk is much higher than for a European city break.
