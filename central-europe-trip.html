Fast itinerary

Six countries in thirteen days is demanding. The highest-risk legs are Zakopane-Krakow-Prague and the Budapest-Ljubljana return. Keep the final Budapest night; do not attempt Ljubljana and the UK flight on the same day.

Day-by-day route

The complete travel plan

DAY 1

Newcastle → Amsterdam → Krakow

Fly on one ticket, take the airport train to Krakow Glowny, check in and walk the Old Town.

Wawel Hill and Kazimierz, then the 17:00-19:00 coach to Zakopane and an evening on Krupowki Street.

Charles Bridge, Prague Castle and Old Town before the 16:01 direct train to Vienna.

City day

Early rail or coach, then the Blue Church, Old Town, Michael's Gate, Danube and Bratislava Castle.

Travel day

City day
