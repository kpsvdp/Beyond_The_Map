Türkiye e-Visa eligibility and application guide

Türkiye is not in the Schengen Area. A Schengen visa does not itself cover entry into Türkiye.

Tourism or business onlyOnline if eligibleSingle entry for one monthNo guaranteed visa on arrival
Official e-Visa portal ↗Trip details
Likely routeConditional e-Visa

You may qualify only if the portal accepts your valid UK, US, Irish or Schengen visa/residence permit and you meet every displayed condition.

Eligibility must be checked for your exact passport and arrival date.
Important

An Indian ordinary-passport holder needs a visa. Do not describe the e-Visa as a visa on arrival and do not book on the assumption that approval is automatic.

Step 1

Check whether you qualify for an e-Visa

The official application screen gives the final answer.

01

Indian passport

Ordinary, special and service passport holders require a visa. Enter the passport type exactly.

02

Valid supporting document

You normally need a valid visa or residence permit from a Schengen country, the USA, the UK or Ireland. A separate country's e-Visa is not accepted as the supporting document.

03

Valid on arrival

Your UK visa or residence permission must still be valid on the date you enter Türkiye. Carry accessible proof of your UK immigration status.

04

Tourism or business

The e-Visa is not valid for work, study or residence. Those purposes require the appropriate consular visa.

05

Portal conditions

The application may require an eligible airline, return/onward ticket, hotel booking and sufficient funds. Accept only if every answer is true.

06

Passport validity

The legal minimum is at least 60 days beyond the permitted stay. Türkiye's Ministry of Foreign Affairs advises at least six months' validity from arrival.

Online route

How to apply

  1. Open only the government portal

    Use evisa.gov.tr ↗. Avoid agents charging unnecessary service fees.

  2. Start a new application

    Select India, the correct passport type and your planned arrival date.

  3. Read every condition

    If the portal says you are not eligible, stop. False answers can invalidate the visa and cause refusal at boarding or the border.

  4. Enter the details exactly

    Your name, passport number, dates and contact information must match the passport.

  5. Verify your email and pay

    Use the link sent by the portal. The official fee is shown before payment and is paid online in US dollars using an accepted international card.

  6. Download and check the PDF

    Confirm the name, passport number, arrival date, validity and permitted stay. Save an offline copy and carry a printout.

Prepare before applying

Practical checklist

  • Indian passport
  • Valid UK visa or residence permission evidence
  • UK immigration status/share-code evidence if applicable
  • Return or onward flight booking
  • Hotel reservations for all five nights
  • Travel route and arrival date
  • International payment card with 3-D Secure
  • Accessible proof of at least US$50 per day
Six-day funds referenceUS$300

This is a minimum-condition reference, not the actual trip budget of £1,585.

⏱️

Can it take only minutes?

The official system is designed to be quick after correct information, email approval and successful payment. That is not a guaranteed decision time. Apply at least 48 hours before departure, not at the airport.

🩺

Is health insurance uploaded?

An eligible e-Visa application does not normally ask you to upload an insurance certificate. Still, travelling uninsured is a bad financial decision. Sticker-visa requirements can be different.

🏦

Is financial proof unnecessary?

No. You may not upload bank statements during the e-Visa form, but the conditions may require sufficient funds, a hotel reservation and a return ticket. Carry evidence in case the airline or border officer asks.

🛬

Is it a visa on arrival?

No. Apply online, receive the approved e-Visa and check it before travel. Payment alone is not permission to enter, and border authorities make the final admission decision.

Fallback route

If the e-Visa portal says you are not eligible

Sticker visa pre-application ↗

Use the official sticker-visa pre-application system and follow the instructions for the Turkish mission or authorised visa centre serving your UK address. The online pre-application is not the end of the process: you may need an appointment and original documents.

Identity and UK status

  • Passport and copies
  • UK residence/visa evidence
  • Application form and photographs

Trip evidence

  • Flight itinerary and hotel bookings
  • Six-day route and cover letter
  • Travel insurance if required

Financial evidence

  • Recent bank statements
  • Payslips and employment letter
  • Savings or sponsor evidence if applicable

Appointment

  • Upload requested documents
  • Attend the correct mission/centre
  • Submit originals and passport

The exact checklist, fee and appointment route can change. Follow the documents generated by the official portal for your case.
