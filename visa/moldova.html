Visa plan

Status: Moldova's official rules exempt otherwise visa-required nationals who hold a valid UK residence permit or valid UK visa, excluding transit visas, for travel during that document's validity.

  • I will carry clear proof of my current UK eVisa/residence permission

    and its validity, not only a screenshot with no verification route.

  • If I do not hold a qualifying UK document, I will apply for Moldova's

    eVisa; the official portal lists a €40 eVisa cost.

  • For either route I will carry hotel, return flight, insurance and

    enough funds; Moldova's short-stay guidance uses at least €30 per day and not less than €300 for stays under 10 days when a visa application is required.

Visa budget: £0 with a qualifying UK document; otherwise allow about £35–£55 for the €40 eVisa and payment variation.
