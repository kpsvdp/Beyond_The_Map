🇸🇪 Sweden · secondary destination

Include Sweden in the Italy file

Three Stockholm nights and the Schengen exit belong in the same Italy-led application.

Do not apply separately

Use the Italy application and show Sweden as the final Schengen stay and exit country.

Swedish proof to add

Stockholm and exit

  • Palma–Stockholm flight with the after-midnight arrival date clearly shown.
  • Generator Stockholm for all 3 nights and written late-check-in confirmation.
  • Arlanda-to-city transport plan and Stockholm itinerary.
  • Stockholm–Krakow flight, separate-booking details and onward Newcastle flight.
  • Explain the unprotected self-transfer and show enough time and money for disruption.
  • Insurance valid through the final Schengen exit.
If the itinerary changes

Use Sweden only if it becomes competent

If Sweden later becomes the longest stay or main purpose, re-check the Swedish official UK route. The Embassy currently directs UK applicants to its authorised VFS process.
