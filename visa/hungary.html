Country route

How to apply if competent

Start with the Hungary VFS UK portal and the official Hungarian application forms. Select short-stay tourism and follow the live centre checklist.

Financial evidence

Bank and occupation proof

Provide the latest three months of UK personal current-account statements showing your name, address, transactions and enough stable funds for the whole trip and return. The UK checklist does not publish a guaranteed daily figure. If employed, add an official employer letter and three months of payslips.

Common documents

Core Schengen file

1. Form and appointment

Signed tourism application, receipt/checklist and appointment confirmation.

2. Passport

Issued within 10 years, at least two blank pages and valid at least three months after leaving Schengen.

3. UK status

Printed eVisa/status evidence and a fresh share code where the mission requests it. The status must permit return to the UK.

4. Complete route

Newcastle-Krakow-Zakopane-Prague-Vienna-Bratislava-Budapest-Ljubljana-Budapest-Newcastle.

5. Every room night

Applicant name, address, dates and reference for all 12 nights, including the final Budapest night.

6. Insurance

All Schengen states, full trip, at least €30,000 for emergency care and repatriation including death.

7. Financial proof

Latest three months of current-account statements. Add savings only when those funds will pay for the trip.

8. Work or study ties

Employer leave letter and payslips, or a recent institution letter with enrolment and approved holiday/absence.

9. Cover letter

One letter explaining all six countries, why Hungary is competent, funding and the return to the UK.

10. Copies and history

Photo, passport copies, previous visas and any sponsor or unusual-deposit evidence that actually applies.

Money warning

No magic balance

  • The balance must cover unpaid trip costs and normal monthly bills.
  • Do not borrow money temporarily or add an unexplained large deposit.
  • Do not rely on a credit-card limit as the main savings evidence.
  • If sponsored, include the sponsor letter, statements, passport/status and relationship proof.
