Short Stay C visa

Applicant profile: Indian passport holder legally resident in the United Kingdom. Rules and appointments must be checked again for the actual travel dates.

Visa plan

Status: Ireland is not in Schengen. An Indian citizen living long-term in the UK normally needs an Irish Short Stay 'C' visa. The UK short-stay visa-waiver programme does not cover long-term UK student, work or residence permission.

  • I will apply online through AVATS, print and sign the summary form,

    and follow the London visa office's submission instructions.

  • I will include an application letter, passport, UK residence proof,

    return travel and hotel evidence, travel insurance, employment/student evidence and six months of bank statements as requested in the Irish short-stay guidance.

  • The current fee is €60 for single entry or €100 for multiple entry,

    plus any document, postage or service costs.

Visa budget: Allow £55–£90 for a single-entry application and submission costs; more for multiple entry.
