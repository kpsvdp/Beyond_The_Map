Consular visa

Applicant profile: Indian passport holder legally resident in the United Kingdom. Rules and appointments must be checked again for the actual travel dates.

Visa plan

Status: A visa is required for Algeria. A standard Algiers city trip does not qualify for the limited southern-tour visa-on-arrival arrangements described by UK travel guidance.

  • I will apply through the Algerian Consulate in the UK before booking

    non-refundable travel and confirm the current fee for an Indian passport holder.

  • I will prepare a passport valid at least six months after arrival,

    forms/photos, proof of UK residence, hotel or invitation, return travel, insurance, bank/income evidence and itinerary.

  • I will recheck the FCDO map immediately before travel because warnings

    apply to several Algerian border areas and insurance can be invalid in advised-against regions.

Visa budget: The consulate sets the nationality-based fee; I will keep a £50–£100 administration/postage reserve until the fee is confirmed.
