🇮🇹 Italy · main destination

Use Italy for this four-country application

Five Italy nights make Italy the competent country. One application covers Switzerland, Spain and Sweden when the complete route is disclosed.

Submit here

Start with the official Visa for Italy information and book only through Italy VFS Global UK.

Core requirements

Prepare the file

  • Signed form, appointment confirmation and €90 adult fee plus live service charge.
  • Passport issued within 10 years, valid 3 months beyond Schengen exit and with 2 blank pages.
  • UK eVisa/status evidence and fresh share-code proof that allows return.
  • All 13 nights and every flight, train and ferry leg.
  • Travel medical insurance of at least €30,000 covering paragliding and scuba.
  • Recent personal bank statements, 3 payslips if employed, and truthful work/study or sponsor evidence.
Financial proof

Do not use a fake minimum

  • Italian 14-day reference calculation: €565.02.
  • Real all-in trip target: £2,975–£3,725.
  • The reference is not a guaranteed balance. Show stable funds for unpaid costs and normal UK commitments.
  • Do not treat a credit-card limit as savings.
