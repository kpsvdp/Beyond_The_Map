Financial proof

Belgium reference amounts

  • Hotel stay: at least €95 per day.
  • Private host: at least €45 per day.
  • Evidence: recent bank statements covering at least the previous 3 months; explain significant recent deposits.
  • Employment: contract or employment certificate and payslips covering at least 3 months.
  • For this day trip: carry credible day funds and a confirmed return ticket; these Belgium figures are not added to the France reference balance.

Belgium arrival declaration

Belgium requires third-country nationals staying at a non-hotel address to report their presence within three working days; hotel guests are exempt. For this same-day visit with no Belgian accommodation, check the official MAB page if any declaration is needed.
