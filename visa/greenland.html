A standard Schengen visa does not automatically cover Greenland. State Greenland in the Danish application and obtain the required territorial validity.

Visa plan

Status: Greenland is outside Schengen. A normal Schengen visa is not valid for Greenland. As an Indian passport holder resident in the UK, I will normally need a visa specifically valid for Greenland and also Schengen permission for the Copenhagen transit/stay.

  • I will apply through the Danish route and tell the application centre

    explicitly that I need Greenland plus Schengen. Denmark states that one application can cover both when declared.

  • I will check the visa sticker wording before travel; it must include

    Greenland rather than only the Schengen states.

  • I will submit flexible flight and hotel evidence because weather

    disruption is a real possibility, and I will buy insurance that actually covers Greenland and the planned boat/snow activity.

Visa budget: Allow roughly £105–£145 for the visa/service process, but confirm the exact combined Greenland/Schengen fee before submission.
