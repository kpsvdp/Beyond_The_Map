Visa plan

Status: Indian ordinary-passport holders normally require a Serbian visa, but Serbia allows visa-free entry for travellers holding a valid UK visa, valid Schengen/EU/US visa, or qualifying Schengen/EU/US residence permit, within the document's validity.
