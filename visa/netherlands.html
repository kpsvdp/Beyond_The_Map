If Netherlands becomes main

Application route in the UK

  1. Confirm competence. The Netherlands must genuinely be the longest stay or main purpose.
  2. Prepare the official checklist. Complete the Schengen form and collect the tourism documents.
  3. Book VFS Global. UK centres include Birmingham, Edinburgh, London and Manchester.
  4. Attend personally. Submit documents and passport, provide fingerprints if required, answer questions and pay fees.
  5. Track with the reference number. Check the visa details immediately after the passport is returned.
Timing and funds

Dutch-specific figures

  • Appointment window: up to 6 months ahead. The official UK page advises applying no later than 45 days before travel.
  • Funds: at least €55 per person for each day. Three calendar days in this plan = €165 reference.
  • Centres: Birmingham, Edinburgh, London or Manchester, for UK residents.
  • In person: attendance is required even for applicants under 18.
Dutch tourism checklist

Main documents if the route changes

Passport

No more than 10 years old, signed, 2 empty visa pages and valid at least 3 months after leaving Schengen.

UK status

Residence evidence plus UKVI eVisa printout and share-code letter. Dutch guidance says the UK status must remain valid at least 1 month after leaving Schengen.

Employee

Recent signed employer letter with role, salary, company details and signer details, plus payslips for the last 3 months.

Student

Recent signed institution letter showing studies, study hours and attendance record.

Accommodation

Hotel reservation for the whole trip, or the appropriate tour/private-accommodation proof.

Money

Personal bank statements from the last 3 months; add sponsor relationship and sponsor evidence where applicable.

Travel

Return to the UK or confirmed onward travel, plus the complete multi-country plan.

Insurance

Named cover for every Schengen country and the whole stay, at least €30,000, including emergency care and repatriation including death.

Financial reality

€55/day is not the whole budget

  • Reference: €165 for 3 calendar days.
  • Also show: enough accessible funds for unpaid rooms, rail, flight, food and normal commitments.
  • Sponsor: if the applicant cannot show €55/day, use the formal sponsorship route and required evidence.
  • Current itinerary: these Dutch funds are a reference only; do not add a separate Dutch balance to the France-led application.
