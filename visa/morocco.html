Visa plan

Status: An Indian passport holder with a qualifying UK residence permit can use Morocco's eVisa route. The permit must meet the official validity condition at the time of application; the published conditions state at least 180 days for the residence permit.

  • I will apply only through the official Accès Maroc portal and upload

    my ordinary passport and qualifying UK residence evidence.

  • The official conditions describe a single-entry eVisa valid up to 180

    days from issue for a stay up to 30 days, with standard and express processing targets in Moroccan working days.

  • I will confirm the live fee on the portal before payment and carry the

    eVisa, hotel, return flight and proof of funds/UK return permission.

Visa budget: Allow roughly £60–£100 until the portal displays the exact standard or express eVisa fee.
